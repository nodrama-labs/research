● regimes.nodrama.io / BTC market-regime model

Bitcoin trades in bear, ranging & bull regimes — a hidden Markov model reads them off the drawdown.

Feed a 3-state HMM one strictly-causal number — how far price sits below its running all-time high — and the latent states line up with what a trader would call a bear, a chop, and a bull. No look-ahead, no hand-labelling.

Method

The observation is , a causal drawdown from the running maximum of log price. A Gaussian HMM is fit by Baum–Welch with five random restarts; states are named by ascending mean. Student's-t emissions were tested and collapse to Gaussian at this feature scale (ν saturates), so K=3 Gaussian is the shipped model. Everything on this page is rendered from a single feed.json — swap the file, ship a new model.
